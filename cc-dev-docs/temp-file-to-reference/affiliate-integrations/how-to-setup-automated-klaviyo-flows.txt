To get affiliates/influencers/partners to launch co-branded shops with your brand, they first need to become aware of the opportunity. While there are other strategies you can leverage for issuing invitations, your primary Klaviyo flow will drive most of your adoption, and is extremely important to enable before updating your relevant programs/tiers for CreatorCommerce. The article will walk you through setting up this flow in Klaviyo. If you don't want to read it and would rather get some hands on support, you can make a request for the CreatorCommerce team to do this for you here.

Step 1: Get the email template
The CreatorCommerce team will provide you with the email templates and flows the correspond to them. Fill out this form to get them.

Template contents
Brand Logo/Header - Set to your brand logo
H1 Title - Showcasing main topic of the email
Body Copy Section 1 - Set the main message of the email
CTA/Button - Set to the main CTA such as the onboarding link or shop link
Body Copy Section 2 - reminder copy
(Optional) Promotional/teaser Image - Shows off what a shop looks like - Contact us to get one for your brand

Step 2: Understanding the email flows
CreatorCommerce will provide your with 8 email flows. This will take you through what each flow is for, why affiliates enter each one, and how they leave each one. Make sure to review each for yourself in your Klaviyo, and make edits where needed. These flows will be "production-ready", and can go live after your review.

Find the flows
Go to Flows
Select the tag "CreatorCommerce"

[CC TEMPLATE] CreatorCommerce - Collab Invitation
To inform the affiliate of the new new affiliate program, and what to do next.

Trigger
"Creator Collaboration Started" - When CreatorCommerce syncs affiliate from the integrated affiliate management platform.

Exit point
"Creator Drop Created"

OR

When the flow finishes all emails in the chain.

‚Äç

[CC TEMPLATE] CreatorCommerce - Welcome Series
To welcome the affiliate to the new program, and to get them excited and informed about the co-selling experience.

Trigger
"Creator Drop Created" - Products are added to the co-branded shop for the first time ever.

Exit point
"Creator Order Attributed" - Affiliate makes a sale.

OR

When the flow finishes all emails in the chain.

‚Äç

[CC TEMPLATE] CreatorCommerce - New Drop
To remind affiliate to share their recent product recommendation.

Trigger
"Creator Drop Created" - Affiliate adds product to their shop.

Exit point
When the only email in the chain is sent.

‚Äç

[CC TEMPLATE] CreatorCommerce - Onboarding Abandonment
To re-engage affiliates who start to setup and customize their shop, but never finish.

Trigger
"Creator Invitation Accepted" - Affiliate selects "Accept" when customizing their shop, followed by no "Creator Drop Created" over the next 4 hours.

Exit point
"Creator Drop Created" - Products are added to the co-branded shop for the first time ever.

OR

When the flow finishes all emails in the chain.

‚Äç

[CC TEMPLATE] CreatorCommerce - Sales Milestones
To add a layer of gamification to the program and congratulate the affiliate on first sale, 10th sale, and all sales in-between.

Trigger
"Creator Order Attributed" - When affiliate makes a sale.

Exit point
When the only email in the chain is sent.

‚Äç

[CC TEMPLATE] CreatorCommerce - Shop Abandoned
To re-engage affiliates who had high intent to participate in the program, and went as far as setting up their shop - but ultimately did not remain productive.

Trigger
When affiliate is added to the automated segment "Abandoned Creator Shop", which is defined as affiliates with shops who have not made a sale or drop in the last 60 days.

Exit point
"Creator Drop Created"- Products are added to the co-branded shop.

OR

"Creator Order Attributed" - Affiliate makes a sale.

OR

When the flow finishes all emails in the chain.

‚Äç

[CC TEMPLATE] CreatorCommerce - Shop Updated
To remind the affiliate that they made changes, and to reaffirm their new handle if they changed it.

Trigger
"Shop Updated" - When shop settings are saved in the dashboard for the specific shop.

Exit point
When the only email in the chain is sent.

‚Äç

[CC Template] CreatorCommerce - New Campaign
Campaign is used to engage affiliates and notify them of a new campaign you are running that you want them to participate in.

Trigger
"Creator Added To Campaign" - Triggered when a corresponding campaign is enabled, or the start date for an active campaign passes.

Exit points
"Creator Removed From Campaign" - When a corresponding campaign is disabled, or the the end date for a active campaign is passes.

OR

"Creator Added To Campaign" - For if individual is added to another campaign.

OR

When the flow finishes all emails in the chain.

Step 3: Go live with the flows
With each flow, select "Update action statuses" and set everything to "Live".

Note: Before proceeding, you should have already enabled the CreatorCommerce integration for your affiliate/influencer marketing platform of choice, and that platform should also already be connected to Klaviyo. You should also have at least one co-branded page build so that when affiliates get invited, they have a co-branded page to ultimately share with others.
