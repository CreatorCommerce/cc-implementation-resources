Co-branding allows you to keep shoppers in context with the unique hooks & stories that brought them to your site in the first place. People click links for a reason, and co-branding ensures that reason stays right in front of them at all times.

CreatorCommerce is an extension to your affiliate links, not a replacement. The platform powers deep, personalized shopping experiences that replace the traditional method of sending traffic to a one-size-fits all homepage/product page. Any existing affiliate links will be supported.


All experiences are hosted on your Shopify storefront, with your domain. All checkouts are conducted through your Shopify checkout.
