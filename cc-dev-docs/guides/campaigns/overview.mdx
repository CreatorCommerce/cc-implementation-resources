## Campaign Planning Framework

### 1. Pick the Moment
Choose a cultural, seasonal, or product moment that:
- Your partners naturally talk about
- Aligns with your product categories
- Has a clear start and end date

### 2. Design the Experience
Build the co-branded funnel:
- Landing page with campaign theme
- Partner's curated product picks
- Campaign-specific messaging and visuals

### 3. Equip Partners
Give partners everything they need:
- Their personalized campaign page URL
- Shareable assets (images, copy snippets)
- Talking points and content angles

### 4. Activate and Track
Launch coordinated push:
- Partner posts and shares
- Email/SMS to partner audiences
- Track per-partner performance
