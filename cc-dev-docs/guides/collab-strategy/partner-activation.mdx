## 1. Data Collection

### What You Need for Personalization

The co-branded experience is only as good as the data behind it. You need:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Identity Data">
    **What:** Name, photo, bio, social handles
    
    **Why:** Creates the visual and textual personalization
    
    **How to get it:**
    - Application forms
    - Social media profile scraping (where permitted)
    - AI generation from partial data
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Preference Data">
    **What:** Favorite products, style preferences, audience info
    
    **Why:** Drives product recommendations and funnel content
    
    **How to get it:**
    - Preference forms in partner portal
    - Order history analysis
    - AI inference from content
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Content Data">
    **What:** Quotes, testimonials, UGC, reviews
    
    **Why:** Adds authenticity and social proof to their pages
    
    **How to get it:**
    - Direct collection via forms
    - Existing social posts (with permission)
    - AI-generated from other data
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Brand Data">
    **What:** Colors, logos (for advanced partners)
    
    **Why:** Deeper co-branding for premium partners
    
    **How to get it:**
    - Partner portal uploads
    - Extracted from existing sites
    - AI color matching from photos
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Collection Methods

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Forms">
    **When to use:** Best for intentional, structured data collection
    
    **Implementation:**
    - Onboarding questionnaires
    - Progressive profiling (ask more over time)
    - Quick-edit fields in partner dashboard
    
    **Tips:**
    - Keep forms short and focused
    - Explain why you're asking each question
    - Allow skipping with "we'll figure it out" fallback
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Order History">
    **When to use:** Partners who are also customers
    
    **Implementation:**
    - Analyze past purchases to infer preferences
    - Pre-populate recommended products
    - Build "favorites" lists automatically
    
    **Tips:**
    - Ask for confirmation, don't just assume
    - Give partners ability to edit/override
    - Use recency and frequency for relevance
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="AI Generation">
    **When to use:** Filling gaps from partial data
    
    **Implementation:**
    - Generate bios from social profile data
    - Create product descriptions in partner's voice
    - Suggest preferences based on audience analysis
    
    **Tips:**
    - Always mark AI-generated content for review
    - Give partners easy editing tools
    - Improve prompts based on feedback
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Social Import">
    **When to use:** Getting existing content/assets
    
    **Implementation:**
    - Import profile photos from social platforms
    - Pull bio text (with permission)
    - Extract UGC that mentions your brand
    
    **Tips:**
    - Be clear about permissions and usage
    - Provide opt-out/delete options
    - Keep content fresh with periodic updates
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## 2. Automated Page Generation

### The Magic Moment

The "shock and awe" comes when a partner sees their personalized page for the first time. This should happen automaticallyâ€”or as close to automatically as possible.

**The flow:**
1. Partner is approved
2. System gathers/generates initial data
3. Co-branded page is created automatically
4. Partner is notified to view and customize

### AI + Automation Approach

<Steps>
  <Step title="Template Selection">
    Based on partner segment, select the appropriate co-branded template:
    - Influencer â†’ Visual-first, UGC-heavy template
    - Practitioner â†’ Professional, clinical template
    - Publisher â†’ Content-focused, editorial template
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Data Population">
    Fill template with available data:
    - Partner name, photo, bio
    - Product recommendations (from preferences or AI)
    - Discount code and commission info
    - Dynamic text tokens for personalization
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="AI Enhancement">
    For missing or partial data, use AI to:
    - Generate bio text from social profile
    - Write personalized product descriptions
    - Create section headings in partner's voice
    - Suggest product selections based on audience
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Quality Moderation">
    Before publishing, either:
    - Auto-publish with partner editing capability
    - Queue for human review (for premium partners)
    - Send to partner for approval
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Templates by Segment

| Segment | Template Focus | Key Elements |
|---------|---------------|--------------|
| Influencer | Visual, lifestyle | Large hero image, UGC grid, style picks |
| Practitioner | Professional, clinical | Credentials, patient resources, protocols |
| Affiliate | Performance, conversion | Clear CTAs, discount emphasis, product focus |
| Publisher | Editorial, content | Article integration, embedded products |

## 3. Partner Notification

### Driving to the "Wow" Moment

You've created something amazing. Now make sure they see it.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Email Notification">
    **The reveal email:**
    
    - Subject: Personal, exciting (not transactional)
    - Hero: Screenshot or preview of their page
    - CTA: "View Your Page" (primary) + "Customize" (secondary)
    - Social proof: "Join X partners already earning"
    
    **Timing:** Send immediately upon page creation, or next morning if overnight
    
    **Example subject lines:**
    - "Your [Brand] page is live ðŸŽ‰"
    - "[Partner Name], your storefront is ready"
    - "We built this for youâ€”come see"
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="SMS Notification">
    **When to use:** For time-sensitive or high-priority partners
    
    **Keep it short:**
    ```
    Hey [Name]! Your [Brand] page is live ðŸ™Œ 
    Check it out: [link]
    ```
    
    **Considerations:**
    - Only if partner opted in to SMS
    - Reserve for key moments, don't overuse
    - Include direct link to their page
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="In-App/Dashboard Notification">
    **For partners in your portal:**
    - Prominent "Your page is ready" banner
    - Guided tour on first visit
    - Preview with "Edit" and "Share" buttons
    
    **Make it discoverable:**
    - Don't bury it in navigation
    - Use visual callouts
    - Consider modal on first login
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Activation Flows

Build automated flows that nurture partners to activation:

```
Day 0: Page created â†’ Email: "Your page is live!"
Day 2: If not viewed â†’ Email: "You haven't seen your page yet"
Day 5: If viewed, not shared â†’ Email: "Here's how to start earning"
Day 7: If no activity â†’ Email: "Quick wins to get started"
```

<Tip>
The goal is activation, not annoyance. Track engagement and stop flows when partners become activeâ€”or when they're clearly not interested.

## Whitelisted Meta Ads Strategy

For qualified partners, offer 1-click ad authorization to run whitelisted Meta ads:

### What It Is

Partners authorize your brand to run ads from their accountsâ€”combining your creative/budget with their audience credibility.

### Why Partners Love It

- **No effort required** â€” You handle everything
- **Looks like their content** â€” Appears from their account
- **Revenue potential** â€” They earn when ads convert
- **Builds their brand** â€” Professional, polished campaigns

### Implementation

1. **Eligibility criteria** â€” Define which partners qualify
2. **Authorization flow** â€” 1-click Meta Business authorization
3. **Creative process** â€” You create (often using their UGC)
4. **Revenue share** â€” Clear terms on ad-driven conversions
5. **Performance reporting** â€” Keep partners informed

<Info>
Whitelisted ads often perform 2-3x better than traditional ads because they inherit the trust and engagement of the creator's account.
</Info>

## Moderation at Scale

With automation comes the need for moderation:

### Moderation Strategy

| Content Type | Moderation Approach |
|--------------|---------------------|
| AI-generated bios | Light review, partner can edit |
| Partner-submitted photos | Manual review before publish |
| Product selections | Auto-approve from catalog |
| UGC for ads | Full review required |

### Admin Tools

Build (or use) moderation tools that enable:
- Bulk review queues
- Quick approve/reject actions
- Edit-in-place for quick fixes
- Escalation for edge cases
- Audit trails for compliance
