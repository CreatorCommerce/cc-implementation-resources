Integrate Northbeam with CreatorCommerce for comprehensive attribution including partner-driven conversions.

## What This Integration Does

| CC → Northbeam | Benefit |
|----------------|---------|
| Partner touchpoints | Include partners in attribution models |
| Co-branded conversions | Accurate partner revenue tracking |
| Funnel events | Full journey visibility |

## Use Cases

### Cross-Channel Attribution
Understand how partner traffic interacts with paid, organic, and email in the customer journey.

### Partner Incrementality
Measure the true incremental impact of your partner program.

### Budget Optimization
Allocate spend between paid and partner channels based on real attribution data.

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Northbeam">
    In CC Admin → Integrations → Northbeam, enter your API key.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure pixel">
    Ensure CC pixel and Northbeam pixel are both installed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Map sources">
    Configure CC partners as a source in Northbeam.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify data">
    Check Northbeam for CC partner data appearing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Data Sent

| Event | Properties |
|-------|------------|
| Page View | partner_handle, page_type, session_id |
| Add to Cart | partner_handle, product_ids, cart_value |
| Purchase | partner_handle, order_id, revenue, products |

## Attribution Integration

Northbeam's MTA models can include:
- Partner first-touch
- Partner last-touch
- Partner assist
- Partner influence decay
