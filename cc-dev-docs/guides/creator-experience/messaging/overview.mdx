Keep your partners informed, engaged, and activated through automated messaging.

## Why Partner Messaging Matters

Partners who feel connected perform better:
- **Faster activation** — Onboarding emails drive first actions
- **Better retention** — Regular updates keep partners engaged
- **Higher performance** — Performance nudges drive results

## Messaging Channels

| Channel | Best For | Integration |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| Email | Detailed updates, onboarding | Klaviyo, Attentive |
| SMS | Urgent updates, quick wins | Attentive, Klaviyo |
| In-app | Dashboard notifications | CC Dashboard |

## Message Types

### Transactional
Triggered by partner actions:
- Welcome after signup
- Confirmation after updates
- Order attribution notifications

### Lifecycle
Based on partner journey:
- Onboarding sequences
- Activation reminders
- Milestone celebrations

### Campaigns
Sent to segments:
- Product launch announcements
- Seasonal content requests
- Performance challenges
