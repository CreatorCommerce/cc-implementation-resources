## Activation Reminder Flow

### Incomplete Profile Reminder

**Trigger:** 48 hours, profile < 50% complete

**Content:**
- What's missing
- How to complete
- Time estimate (2 minutes)

### Products Reminder

**Trigger:** Profile complete + 48 hours, no products

**Content:**
- Why product selection matters
- Curated suggestions based on their niche
- Link to product picker

### Share Reminder

**Trigger:** Products selected + 72 hours, no shares

**Content:**
- Their link is ready
- Share suggestions (Instagram bio, stories)
- Sample captions
