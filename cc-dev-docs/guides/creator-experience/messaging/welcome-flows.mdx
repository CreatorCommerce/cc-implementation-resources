Welcome flows set the tone for your partnership. Get partners excited and activated quickly.

## Welcome Sequence Structure

### Day 0: Immediate Welcome

**Trigger:** Partner created

**Content:**
- Welcome to the program
- What to expect
- First action CTA (complete profile)

### Day 1: Profile Nudge

**Trigger:** 24 hours, profile incomplete

**Content:**
- Reminder to complete profile
- Why it matters
- How long it takes

### Day 3: Product Selection

**Trigger:** 72 hours, no products selected

**Content:**
- Encourage product picks
- Show popular choices
- Link to product picker

### Day 7: Share Prompt

**Trigger:** 7 days, no shares/clicks

**Content:**
- Encourage first share
- Provide share assets
- Show what others are doing

## Personalization

Use CC data for personalization:

| Variable | Source | Example |
|----------|--------|---------|
| `firstName` | Partner profile | "Hi Sarah" |
| `shopUrl` | CC-generated | "yourbrand.com/p/sarah" |
| `discountCode` | Partner assignment | "SARAH20" |
| `discountAmount` | Partner tier | "20%" |
