### Monthly Report

**Trigger:** Monthly schedule (1st of month)

**Content:**
- Month recap
- Earnings summary
- Performance trends
- Upcoming opportunities

### Real-Time Order Notifications

**Trigger:** Order attributed

**Content:**
- Order placed notification
- Product(s) purchased
- Commission earned

## Personalization by Tier

| Tier | Frequency | Detail Level |
|------|-----------|--------------|
| Bronze | Monthly | Basic stats |
| Silver | Weekly + Monthly | Full stats |
| Gold | Real-time + Weekly | Full stats + comparisons |
| Platinum | Real-time + Weekly | Full stats + dedicated support |

## Performance Comparisons

Give context without discouraging:

**Do:**
- "You're in the top 25% of partners this month"
- "Your conversion rate beats the average by 15%"
- "You've grown 30% since last month"

**Don't:**
- "You're #247 of 500 partners"
- "Top partners earned $50k, you earned $500"

## Gamification Elements

Add motivation:

- **Streaks:** "3 weeks in a row with sales!"
- **Progress bars:** "80% to Silver tier"
- **Badges:** "Top 10% this month"
- **Leaderboards:** Opt-in only, curated

## Building in Klaviyo

Use CC data synced to Klaviyo:

1. **Create segment:** Partners with orders in last 7 days
2. **Build flow:** Triggered weekly
3. **Add dynamic content:** Pull stats from profile properties
4. **Schedule:** Monday 9am local time
