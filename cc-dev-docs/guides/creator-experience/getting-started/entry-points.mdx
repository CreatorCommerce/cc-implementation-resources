Partners need to access your collection surfaces. Here are the entry points.

## The Flow

```
Partner receives access
        │
        ▼
   Entry Point
   (URL, email, button, account UI)
        │
        ▼
   Auth (SSO, magic link, password)
        │
        ▼
   Collection Surface
   (form, dashboard, picker)
```

## Entry Point Types

### Direct URLs

Simple links to CC-hosted forms and dashboards.

**Use for:**
- Sharing in DMs, Slack, partner portals
- Linking from your website
- QR codes at events

**Example:** `https://partners.yourbrand.com/dashboard`

### Emails with URLs + SSO

Partner emails containing magic links that authenticate automatically.

**Use for:**
- Onboarding sequences
- Update requests
- Reminder campaigns

**Flow:**
1. Partner receives email
2. Clicks magic link
3. Automatically authenticated
4. Lands on form/dashboard

### Embedded Buttons in Partner Apps

Buttons in your existing partner tools that deep-link with SSO.

**Use for:**
- Affiliate platforms (Impact, ShareASale)
- CRMs (HubSpot, Salesforce)
- Custom partner portals

**Flow:**
1. Partner is logged into your app
2. Clicks "Customize My Shop" button
3. SSO passes identity
4. Opens CC dashboard authenticated

### UI in Customer Accounts

Shopify customer account extensions for partners who are also customers.

**Use for:**
- Ambassador programs where partners are customers
- Loyalty → Affiliate upgrades
- Self-service partner management

**Flow:**
1. Customer logs into account
2. Sees "My Partnership" section
3. Accesses forms/dashboard in-account

## Authentication Options

| Method | Best For | Setup |
|--------|----------|-------|
| Magic links | Email-based entry | Built into CC |
| SSO (SAML/OAuth) | Enterprise, existing identity | Configuration required |
| Password | Standalone access | Built into CC |
| Customer account | Shopify-based identity | App block install |
