This glossary lists common ecommerce components plus co-branded patterns unique to creator/affiliate storefronts. Definitions are short so you can scan and map to your theme sections quickly.

## Global Navigation & Utility

- **Header** — Global navigation with logo, search, account, and cart.
- **Announcement bar** — Top strip for promos, shipping, or alerts.
- **USP strip** — Short benefits row (shipping, returns, guarantee).
- **Mega menu** — Expanded nav with categories, promos, and images.
- **Breadcrumbs** — Location trail for page hierarchy.
- **Search bar** — Keyword input for catalog search.
- **Account entry** — Login and account access link.
- **Mini cart** — Slide-out cart summary.
- **Support widget** — Live chat or help CTA.
- **Language/currency switcher** — Locale selection controls.
- **Trust badges** — Security, payments, or compliance marks.
- **Toast notifications** — Short messages for add-to-cart or errors.
- **Skeleton states** — Loading placeholders for async content.

## Home & Landing Pages

- **Hero banner** — Primary headline, value prop, and CTA.
- **Hero carousel** — Rotating hero slides for multiple campaigns.
- **Category grid** — Visual tiles linking to key categories.
- **Featured collection** — Curated group of products.
- **Best sellers** — Highest-performing items for social proof.
- **New arrivals** — Recently added products.
- **Limited-time offer** — Promotion with urgency copy or timer.
- **Brand story** — Short narrative about the brand.
- **Founder note** — Personal voice to build trust.
- **Press logos** — Media mentions for credibility.
- **Testimonials** — Customer quotes and ratings.
- **UGC gallery** — Customer photos and tagged posts.
- **Video feature** — Brand or product overview video.
- **Lookbook** — Styled product groupings.
- **Blog teaser** — Recent content preview.
- **Email signup** — Newsletter capture module.
- **Store locator** — Map or search for retail locations.
- **FAQ teaser** — Link to common questions.

## Collection & Category Pages

- **Collection hero** — Banner with category title and imagery.
- **Collection description** — Short text about the category.
- **Facet sidebar** — Persistent filter panel for deep catalogs.
- **Product grid** — Card layout of products.
- **Product list** — Row layout with quick details.
- **Filters** — Attribute refinements (size, color, price).
- **Sort bar** — Order products by relevance, price, rating.
- **Filter pills** — Active filter chips with remove action.
- **Price slider** — Range selector for price.
- **Swatches** — Visual filter options for colors or patterns.
- **Quick add** — Add to cart from the grid.
- **Quick view** — Modal preview without leaving page.
- **Hover swap** — Secondary image on hover for quick preview.
- **Infinite scroll** — Loads more products on scroll.
- **Pagination** — Page navigation controls.
- **Compare toggle** — Select products to compare.
- **Wishlist button** — Save products for later.
- **Recently viewed** — Recirculation module.

## Product Detail Pages (PDP)

- **Product media gallery** — Images and videos for the item.
- **Video shoppable** — Video with interactive hotspots or add-to-cart.
- **Zoom/lightbox** — Detailed view of media.
- **Product title** — Name of the item.
- **Price block** — Price, compare-at, and discount display.
- **Variant selector** — Size, color, or style choices.
- **Swatch picker** — Visual variant selection.
- **Inventory status** — In stock, low stock, backorder.
- **Shipping estimate** — Delivery window by location.
- **Pickup availability** — In-store pickup eligibility.
- **Delivery scheduling** — Select delivery date/time window.
- **Quantity selector** — Choose number of items.
- **Add to cart** — Primary purchase action.
- **Buy now** — Direct checkout action.
- **Payment badges** — Payment methods and financing.
- **Promo message** — Discount or bundle callout.
- **Subscription option** — Subscribe-and-save toggle.
- **Subscription cadence** — Choose delivery frequency.
- **Bundle builder** — Add complementary products.
- **Product description** — Detailed info and benefits.
- **Specs table** — Dimensions and technical details.
- **Materials & care** — Fabric, ingredients, or care info.
- **Size guide** — Fit and measurement help.
- **Fit recommender** — Size suggestions based on profile/answers.
- **Ingredient highlights** — Key ingredient callouts.
- **How-to block** — Usage steps or routine instructions.
- **Reviews summary** — Star rating and count.
- **Reviews list** — Full customer reviews.
- **Q&A** — Product questions and answers.
- **Social share** — Share to social platforms.
- **Sticky ATC** — Floating purchase bar.
- **Back in stock** — Notify-me capture.
- **Related products** — Cross-sell suggestions.

## Search

- **Autocomplete** — Suggestions while typing.
- **Search results grid** — Matching products display.
- **No results** — Guidance and fallback options.
- **Search filters** — Refinement within results.
- **Search sort** — Ordering controls.

## Cart, Checkout, and Post‑Purchase

- **Cart page** — Full cart with line items.
- **Line item** — Product row with price and quantity.
- **Edit line item** — Change size/color from cart.
- **Remove item** — Delete from cart.
- **Quantity stepper** — Increase/decrease quantity.
- **Cart notes** — Gift or order instructions.
- **Gift wrap** — Add-on service.
- **Discount code** — Apply promo or voucher.
- **Shipping estimator** — Calculate shipping cost.
- **Progress bar** — Free shipping or tiered goal.
- **Cart upsell** — Add-on recommendations in cart.
- **Bundle add-on** — One-click add for curated bundles.
- **Order summary** — Subtotal, shipping, taxes.
- **Checkout CTA** — Proceed to checkout.
- **Express checkout** — Apple Pay, PayPal, Shop Pay.
- **Trust badges** — Security assurances at checkout.
- **Empty cart** — State with recommendations.
- **Order confirmation** — Success message with details.
- **Order tracking** — Shipping status and tracking links.
- **Reorder CTA** — One-click repurchase.

## Popups & Overlays

- **Email capture** — Newsletter signup modal.
- **Exit intent** — Offer when user leaves.
- **Welcome offer** — First-time discount modal.
- **Cart upsell** — Prompt to add more items.
- **Back in stock** — Notify modal.
- **Age gate** — Verification for restricted goods.
- **Cookie consent** — Privacy notice.
- **Survey** — Quick feedback capture.

## Account & Loyalty

- **Login/signup** — Account creation and access.
- **Order history** — Past purchases list.
- **Order detail** — Individual order view.
- **Address book** — Saved shipping addresses.
- **Payment methods** — Stored payment options.
- **Returns portal** — Start a return or exchange.
- **Loyalty dashboard** — Points and tier status.
- **Referral program** — Invite friends and earn.
- **Subscription management** — Edit recurring orders.

## Co‑Branded Storefront Components (Creator/Affiliate‑Specific)

### Identity & Attribution

- **Co‑brand lockup** — Brand + creator logos paired in one block.
- **Creator header** — Name, photo, and creator handle at top.
- **Creator bio** — Short narrative about the partner.
- **Creator badge** — Verified/featured status indicator.
- **Attribution badge** — Shows shopping via a specific creator.
- **Share‑credit label** — Displays creator code or handle credit.
- **Creator storefront label** — Subtle “You’re shopping [Creator]’s store” label.
- **Creator CTA** — “Shop [Creator]’s picks” call‑to‑action.

### Co‑Branded Messaging

- **Dynamic headline** — Title personalized to creator context.
- **Creator quote callout** — Short endorsement block.
- **Personal note** — Creator intro or welcome message.
- **Co‑branded banner** — Brand‑approved banner with creator info.
- **Affiliate disclosure** — Clear disclosure of partnership.
- **Creator FAQ** — Creator-specific questions and answers.

### Creator Merchandising

- **Creator picks rail** — Curated product carousel.
- **Creator collection grid** — Products chosen by the creator.
- **Creator drop module** — Limited drop with creator branding.
- **Drop countdown** — Timer for launch or closing window.
- **Creator bundle** — Bundled products curated by creator.
- **Shop the routine** — Step‑by‑step product stack.
- **Creator kit list** — Set of tools/products for a routine or project.
- **Before/after block** — Results or transformation showcase.
- **Creator recommendation badges** — “Approved by [Name]” tags.

### Pricing & Offers

- **Creator discount badge** — Per‑creator price callout.
- **Applied creator code** — Visible code confirmation.
- **Exclusive offer card** — Creator‑only deal tile.
- **Tiered discount display** — Different rates by creator tier.
- **Referral unlock** — Unlock discount after referral action.
- **Creator gift-with-purchase** — Bonus item tied to creator purchase.

### Content & Social Proof

- **Creator UGC gallery** — Photos/videos from creator.
- **Creator review block** — Long‑form review content.
- **Creator Q&A** — Creator answers product questions.
- **Creator tips** — Usage advice or styling notes.
- **Routine/playlist embed** — Embedded content tied to creator.
- **Creator demo video** — Instructional or review video by creator.

### Navigation & Discovery

- **Creator directory entry** — Card for a creator in a directory.
- **Creator directory filters** — Filter by niche, audience, tier.
- **Related creators** — Similar creator suggestions.
- **Creator breadcrumb** — Shows route from creator page to product.
- **Back to creator storefront** — Return link on PDP and cart.
- **Creator tag filter** — Filter by creator tag on collection pages.

### Cart & Checkout Co‑Branding

- **Creator cart banner** — Creator strip in cart.
- **Attribution summary** — Shows credited creator before checkout.
- **Creator thank‑you block** — Post‑purchase creator message.
- **Creator donation add‑on** — Optional cause or creator support.
- **Creator reorder nudge** — Remind to restock via creator link.

### Operational UI

- **Destination badge** — Shows which experience path is active.
- **Context resolver state** — UI for “creator not found” fallbacks.
- **Default storefront fallback** — Standard experience when no creator.
- **Tracking indicator** — Confirms creator tracking is active.

## Category‑Specific Components

### Fashion & Apparel

- **Fit notes** — Creator sizing guidance by product.
- **Size matrix** — Size table by region or body measurements.
- **Complete the look** — Outfit pairing carousel.
- **Styling tips** — How-to-wear suggestions.

### Health & Wellness

- **Routine builder** — Step-by-step regimen stack.
- **Subscription cadence** — Refill frequency selector.
- **Ingredient callouts** — Benefit-focused ingredient panel.
- **Usage compliance** — Dosage and timing guidance.

### Beauty & Cosmetics

- **Shade finder** — Quiz or filter for shade matching.
- **Before/after slider** — Visual results comparison.
- **Finish/coverage chips** — Tags for texture and coverage.
- **Routine order** — Sequence for application steps.

### Home & Garden

- **Room scene gallery** — Lifestyle shots in context.
- **Project checklist** — Step-by-step build or care list.
- **Care calendar** — Seasonal maintenance schedule.
- **Delivery constraints** — Shipping/assembly notes.

### Food & Beverage

- **Flavor notes** — Tasting profile and descriptors.
- **Nutrition panel** — Key nutrition facts and highlights.
- **Dietary badges** — Vegan, gluten‑free, keto, etc.
- **Serving suggestions** — Pairings and use ideas.
- **Batch/roast date** — Freshness indicator for perishable goods.
- **Subscription cadence** — Refill frequency selector.

## Quick Mapping Tips

- Use the **Global** and **PDP** sections as your baseline theme inventory.
- Layer **Co‑Branded Storefront Components** on top where creator context exists.
- If a component needs creator data, verify it works in both **creator present** and **creator absent** states.
