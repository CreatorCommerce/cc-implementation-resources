## The Full Picture

CreatorCommerce isn't a single feature — it's infrastructure for building an owned creator-commerce channel. Here's every capability across the lifecycle, from recruiting a creator to converting their audience.

## Common Merchant Operations

Use these workflows when a creator already exists in CreatorCommerce and you need to help them get started, test their storefront, or understand what powers the experience.

### Get a Creator's Invite or Onboarding Link

1. Make sure the creator is assigned to a valid [Destination](/guides/storefronts/destinations-explained). If the creator is not in a Destination, CreatorCommerce does not treat them as active for storefront and onboarding workflows.
2. Open the **Collaborations** table.
3. Click the right side of the creator's row, or open the creator directly.
4. Click to copy the creator's invite/onboarding link.
5. Send the link to the creator. The link signs them in automatically and takes them into the right onboarding or dashboard experience.

Creators can also get into onboarding themselves through a Klaviyo creator invite flow, the CreatorCommerce customer account block, or an embedded sign-in integration inside your affiliate platform's affiliate portal. For the auth model, see [Links + Auth Explained](/guides/creator-experience/forms/links-auth).

### Test or View a Creator's Storefront

1. Find the creator's collab in CreatorCommerce.
2. Click the collab's affiliate link.
3. Confirm the affiliate link routes through `/apps/cc-storefront/redirect`.

That redirect is the essential integration step for affiliate links. It lets CreatorCommerce resolve the creator, apply the right Destination, append creator context, and load any Destination-specific template such as `?view=template-name`.

If the affiliate platform is not yet sending traffic through the redirect, you can manually preview a creator page with:

```text
/pages/creator/{{handle}}?cc={{handle}}
```

Get the handle from the creator's page in CreatorCommerce. Use this as a preview fallback only; production affiliate traffic should pass through `/apps/cc-storefront/redirect` so tracking, UTMs, Destinations, and SDK personalization work correctly. See [Affiliate Link Tracking](/references/affiliate-link-tracking) for redirect requirements.

### Understand Where Shoppers Land

CreatorCommerce can render a core creator page at `/pages/creator/{{handle}}`, but the shopper experience can be any page in your Shopify theme. The CC SDK and default CC sections can reference creator data across the theme, so co-branding is not limited to one page.

The mechanism that decides where affiliate traffic goes is the [Destination](/guides/storefronts/destinations-explained). A Destination defines the landing spot for each creator/tier and can add template parameters such as `?view=summer-campaign`.

### Understand Forms

If you do not build any custom forms, creators still have a default onboarding path. You can override onboarding copy, fonts, and imagery in CreatorCommerce settings, then let the default flow take creators through product selection, product reviews or UGC, collection title, and collection description.

Use custom forms when you want custom copy, questions backed by custom fields, required or optional field control, or a repeatable dashboard form. Collection forms are the backbone of product curation: they combine a creator's selected products with collection-level questions, and they can render product forms for per-product notes or reviews. See [CC Forms & Dashboard](/guides/creator-experience/forms/overview) for the full form model.

### Read Metrics

CreatorCommerce dashboard metrics come from the CC tracking layer and web pixel. The web pixel is enabled by default in settings. Session-driven dashboard data is delayed by 24 hours, so data viewed at 2pm ET reflects the state through 2pm ET the previous day. For real-time reporting, use Shopify reports. See [Funnel Analytics](/use-cases/funnel-analytics) and [Enable Tracking on Liquid Themes](/guides/storefronts/enable-tracking).

### Set Up Klaviyo or Another Integration

Klaviyo supports two jobs: co-branded customer emails such as cart abandonment and post-purchase flows, and creator lifecycle emails such as onboarding invites, referral milestones, and campaign participation nudges. Add your Klaviyo API key in CreatorCommerce, then email [help@creatorcommerce.shop](mailto:help@creatorcommerce.shop) to have the base emails and flows assembled. Invite [team@creatorcommerce.shop](mailto:team@creatorcommerce.shop) as a Klaviyo manager when you need CreatorCommerce to help build flows.

For any other integration, open the integration card in CreatorCommerce, click **Learn more**, and follow the integration-specific setup. If you need help, [book time with CreatorCommerce](https://cal.com/creatorcommerce/30min) or email [help@creatorcommerce.shop](mailto:help@creatorcommerce.shop).

## Co-branded Storefronts

The core of CreatorCommerce — dynamic, personalized shopping experiences.

| Capability | What It Does |
|------------|-------------|
| **Landing pages** | Creator-branded landing pages built on Shopify metaobject templates with the creator's name, photo, bio, and curated products |
| **Homepage and PDP alterations** | Personalize any page across the Shopify theme when a creator's audience is browsing — creator quotes on product pages, dynamic banners, custom pricing |
| **Content injection** | Pull creator content (UGC, notes, media) into any section across the funnel |
| **Dynamic discounts** | Display creator-specific discounted prices across the entire theme with automatic checkout application |
| **Curated drops and bundles** | Creator-curated product collections with titles, descriptions, and product-level enhancement notes |
| **Custom field display** | Surface custom data collected from creators — specialties, credentials, product testimonials, sizing notes — anywhere on the storefront using Liquid templates |
| **Multiple template variations** | Create different landing page layouts for different campaigns or creator tiers using Shopify's template system |
| **Full-funnel personalization** | Extend co-branding from landing page through product pages, cart, checkout, and post-purchase |

## Tracking and Attribution

Measure the full funnel from click to conversion.

| Capability | What It Does |
|------------|-------------|
| **Cart-based tracking** | CreatorCommerce uses cart attributes for tracking — the most reliable method in the Shopify ecosystem, capturing ~2.5% more orders than cookies and codes alone |
| **Step funnel analytics** | See performance at every stage: landing → product view → add to cart → checkout → purchase |
| **Order and customer tagging** | Automatic Shopify tags on orders (`CreatorCommerce`, `cc-creator-id-*`, `cc-campaign-id-*`) and customers for filtering and reporting |
| **Affiliate link tracking** | Transparent pass-through of tracking parameters — CreatorCommerce appends its own tracking without modifying original affiliate platform parameters |
